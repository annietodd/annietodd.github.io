Where in the world is Harmony Group?

Examining Harmony Group's properties

By Annie Todd

Developer Uri Kaufman has been working in the New York development industry since the 1990s. During this time, he has converted historical buildings into loft apartments, self-storage areas and medical offices.

"We are the poster child of how adaptive reuse of historic buildings can revitalize the life of New York." Uri Kaufman"

His newest project is converting the Rockaway Courthouse into doctor offices. The project begain in 2013 and will hopefully be finished by the fall of 2019.

"We’re very excited that the project is finally moving forward," Kaufman said. "We think it’ll be great for the community."
